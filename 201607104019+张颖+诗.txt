<h1><p><b><&lt锄禾&gt></b></p></h1>
<p>&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp锄禾日当午，</p>
<p>&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp汗滴禾下土。</p>
<p>&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp谁知盘中餐，</p>
<p>&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp粒粒皆辛苦。</p>
<h1><p><b><&lt静夜思&gt></b></p></h1>
<p>&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp床前明月光,</p>
<p>&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp疑是地上霜。</p>
<p>&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp举头望明月，</p>
<p>&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp低头思故乡。</p>
